Reboot

Restarts DigiHub. Whatever mode is active (Direwolf, WSJT-X/ JS8Call/qSSTV, Winlink, ARDOP, WebChat) stops, and this page will be unreachable for a minute or two while it comes back up.

Shut Down

Powers DigiHub off completely. There is no way to turn it back on from here — you'll need to physically power-cycle the device.

Reset Configuration

Deletes the callsign/license/location/radio configuration (.dhinfo) and its backup, leaving DigiHub itself — scripts, packages, systemd units — fully installed but unconfigured, the same state install.sh leaves it in before setup. This cannot be undone.
